DROBU, PRESS / MEDIA KIT
=========================

One-liner
  The clipboard you can edit.

Short description (≈30 words)
  Drobu is a macOS clipboard manager that captures everything you copy, text,
  images, GIFs, files, even a screen recording, into one searchable history you
  can crop, trim, clean, and paste from with a keystroke.

Description (≈150 words)
  Drobu is a macOS clipboard manager built around one idea: the clipboard should
  be where the work happens, not just where things sit. Everything you copy lands
  in one searchable history, text, links, images, GIFs, files, even a region of
  your screen you record on the spot. You crop a screenshot, trim a clip, or clean
  up copied text right in the panel, then paste one item or a whole stack with a
  single keystroke. No app-switching, no exports.
  Drobu runs entirely on your Mac. There is no account to create, no tracking, and
  your clipboard never leaves your device; password-manager items are ignored
  automatically. It is a one-time $14.99 purchase (not a subscription) with a
  14-day free trial that needs no card, and it runs on macOS 14 and later.

What Drobu is NOT
  - Not a subscription (one-time $14.99).
  - Not a cloud or sync service, it is local-only; nothing you copy is uploaded.
  - Not an account-based app, you can use it without signing up for anything.
  - Not the Mac App Store build, it ships as a notarized direct download.
  - Not "Drobu Media", "Drobių Namai", or any canvas-print / insurance company.
    Drobu is the macOS clipboard manager at https://drobu.app.
